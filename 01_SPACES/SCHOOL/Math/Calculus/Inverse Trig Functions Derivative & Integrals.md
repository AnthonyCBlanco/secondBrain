### Idea
Derivative of $\arcsin$: $\frac{1}{\sqrt{ 1-x^2 }}$ 
Integral of $\frac{1}{\sqrt{ 1-x^2 }}$: $\arcsin$
or a more useful formula
$$
\int \frac{1}{\sqrt{ a^2-x^2 }}= \arcsin\left( \frac{x}{a} \right)
$$
For Cosine
$$
\frac{d}{dx}\left[ \arccos\left( \frac{x}{a} \right) \right]=\frac{-1}{\sqrt{ a^2-x^2 }}
$$
For Tangent
$$
\frac{d}{dx} \frac{1}{a} \left[ \arctan \left( \frac{x}{a} \right) \right]=\frac{1}{a^2+x^2}
$$
For Secant
$$
\frac{d}{dx}[\sec^{-1}|x|]=\frac{1}{|x|\sqrt{ x^2-1 }}
$$
